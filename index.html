Our Philosophy

At CHW, we believe that our bodies already contain blueprints for wellness in our cycles and our female physiology. We are dedicated to supporting women to discover the peace and power that comes from observing and listening to our bodies' cues and rhythms.

Natural Rhythms

Embracing the wisdom of your menstrual cycle and life seasons as sources of power and guidance for holistic wellness.

Community Care

Building support systems and sacred sisterhood connections that nurture and uplift every woman through all stages of life.

Holistic Wellness

Honoring the interconnection of mind, body, and spirit in your journey toward complete wholeness and wellbeing.
