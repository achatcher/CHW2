Our Mission

At CHW, we are dedicated to the care of women's hearts, souls, minds, and bodies. We want women to discover the peace and power that comes from observing and listening to our bodies' cues and rhythms.

Our Philosophy

We believe that fertility and childbirth are at the crux of all women's health and wellbeing. We believe in a "community care" model, as we are always striving to grow support systems and community groups for women to thrive.

Our approach is holistic and natural, as we believe that our bodies already contain blueprints for wellness in our cycles and our female physiology.

Are you ready to:

  • Learn about and embrace your cycles and natural rhythms?
  • Connect with your intuition and embrace the wild woman within?
  • Support your fertility and hormones naturally?
  • Connect with like-minded women for sisterhood?
  • Embrace the wisdom of your monthly cycle or menopause?

About Krista

I am a mother, healer, writer, dreamer, nurturer, analyst, dancer, creative, explorer, nature lover, facilitator, and aspiring matriarch and wise woman.

My dream is to serve women from "womb to tomb," to build lifelong relationships and community, to share the knowledge that our cycles and rhythms as women are not things to be ignored or feared but embraced.

Our Services

Birth Attendant

Supporting Your Sacred Journey

Support for pregnant women and postpartum care. I believe pregnancy and postpartum care should be holistic, mother-centered, and focused on mindset.

I believe pregnancy and postpartum care should be:

  • Holistic: involving your mental, emotional, spiritual, and physical wellbeing
  • Mother-centered: I serve YOU, the mother, first and foremost
  • Focused on mindset: addressing beliefs and vision for birth and motherhood
  • Involving matrescence: when a baby is born, so is a mother

What I Offer:

  • Holistic pregnancy support and education
  • Birth planning and preparation
  • Postpartum care and meal packages
  • Partner support and education
  • Support for natural, undisturbed birth
  • Homebirth and freebirth assistance
