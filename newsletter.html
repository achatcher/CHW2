A Message from Krista

Dear Beautiful Souls,

As we transition into autumn, I'm reminded of nature's profound wisdom about letting go. The trees don't resist releasing their leaves – they trust in the natural cycle of death and rebirth that makes space for new growth.

This month, I invite you to consider: What are you ready to release? What patterns, beliefs, or habits no longer serve your highest good?

With love and sisterhood,
Krista

Seasonal Wisdom

Honoring Your Inner Autumn

Just as trees release their leaves, we too can learn to let go with grace and trust in our natural cycles.

Autumn invites us to turn inward, to reflect on what has served us and what we're ready to release. This season mirrors our own menstrual cycle's luteal phase – a time of natural decluttering and preparation for renewal.

Autumn Practices for Inner Harmony:

  • Daily gratitude journaling for what you're ready to release
  • Seasonal cleansing rituals for your home and body
  • Warming foods that nourish your root chakra
  • Quiet reflection and meditation practices
  • Connection with other women in sacred circle

Remember, dear one – you are part of nature's rhythm. Trust in your body's wisdom and honor where you are in your unique cycle.
