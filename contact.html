Common Questions

What happens in a discovery call?

We'll have a relaxed 30-minute conversation where I learn about your journey and you can ask questions about my approach. No pressure - just seeing if we're a good fit.

Do you work virtually?

Yes! I offer both in-person support in the Chicago area and virtual sessions worldwide.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's perfectly normal! Many women reach out feeling uncertain. We'll explore together what might be most helpful for your situation.
