Simple Cycle Tracking

Physical Signs

  • Energy levels (1-10 scale)
  • Mood changes
  • Sleep quality
  • Appetite variations
  • Physical symptoms

Emotional Patterns

  • Dominant emotions
  • Social preferences
  • Creativity levels
  • Decision-making clarity
  • Stress responses
Pro Tip: Track for 3 cycles to identify your unique patterns. Use a simple journal, app, or calendar.

Cyclical Nourishment

Menstrual: Iron-rich foods, warm soups, herbal teas, dark chocolate
Follicular: Fresh vegetables, lean proteins, sprouted foods, green tea
Ovulatory: Raw foods, fiber-rich vegetables, anti-inflammatory foods
Luteal: Complex carbs, magnesium-rich foods, roasted vegetables

Cyclical Movement

Menstrual: Gentle yoga, walking, stretching, meditation
Follicular: Light cardio, new workout classes, hiking
Ovulatory: High-intensity workouts, strength training, sports
Luteal: Pilates, moderate exercise, completion-focused activities
