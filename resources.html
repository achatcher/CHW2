Recommended Reading

Books that have shaped our understanding of women's wisdom and natural health

"Taking Charge of Your Fertility"

by Toni Weschler

The definitive guide to natural birth control, pregnancy achievement, and reproductive health.

"Red Tent Sisters"

by Rebecca Booth

Exploring the power of women's circles and menstrual wisdom traditions.

"The Wisdom of Menopause"

by Christiane Northrup

Creating physical and emotional health during the change.

"Spiritual Midwifery"

by Ina May Gaskin

Classic guide to natural childbirth and the power of women's bodies.

"Women's Bodies, Women's Wisdom"

by Christiane Northrup

Creating physical and emotional health and healing.

"The Fourth Trimester"

by Kimberly Ann Johnson

A postpartum guide to healing your body, balancing emotions, and restoring vitality.
