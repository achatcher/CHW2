About CHW

Our Story & Mission

Walking with women from menarche to menopause and beyond through holistic care and sacred community.

Our Mission

At CHW, we are dedicated to the care of women's hearts, souls, minds, and bodies. We want women to discover the peace and power that comes from observing and listening to our bodies' cues and rhythms.

Holistic Care

Supporting your mental, emotional, spiritual, and physical wellbeing as one interconnected whole.

Sacred Community

Building support systems and community groups where women can thrive together.

Natural Wisdom

Honoring your body's natural rhythms and cycles as sources of wisdom and power.

Meet Krista

I believe in the profound wisdom of the feminine cycle and the healing power of community.
