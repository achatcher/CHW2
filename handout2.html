Morning Practices

Start Your Day Grounded
Mindful Awakening (5 min)

Before getting up, take 5 deep breaths and set an intention for your day

Gratitude Practice (3 min)

Name 3 things you're grateful for, including something about your body

Hydration Ritual (2 min)

Drink warm water with lemon while visualizing nourishment flowing through you

Movement Connection (10 min)

Gentle stretching, yoga, or dance to awaken your body with love

Midday Reset

Mindful Eating (During meals)

Eat without distractions, chew slowly, honor your hunger and fullness

Nature Connection (5-15 min)

Step outside, feel your feet on the earth, breathe fresh air

Energy Check-in (2 min)

Ask yourself: "What does my body need right now?" Listen and respond

Boundary Practice

When Stress Arises

ü´Å Immediate Relief (0-5 minutes)

  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8 counts
  • Progressive Muscle Release: Tense and release each muscle group
  • Grounding Technique: Name 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you feel, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Cold Water: Splash on wrists and face or hold ice cubes

üåø Short-term Support (5-30 minutes)

  • Walking Meditation: Slow, mindful steps while focusing on breath
  • Emotional Freedom Technique: Gentle tapping on acupressure points
  • Journaling Stream: Write continuously without editing for 10 minutes
  • Warm Bath/Shower: Let water wash away tension while breathing deeply

üå∏ Long-term Healing (Daily practices)

  • Meditation Practice: Even 5 minutes daily builds resilience
  • Regular Movement: Find joyful ways to move your body
  • Community Connection: Reach out to supportive friends or join circles
  • Nervous System Care: Prioritize sleep, nutrition, and boundary setting
